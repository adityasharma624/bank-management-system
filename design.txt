- "customer" runs the program
- "customer" logs in "account" using "account number" and "pin" (normal pin, hashed afterwards).
- "customer" can access "savings account" and "current account"
    - "savings account" has minimum balance, "current account" does not.
    - "withdraw" and "deposit" is an option for both.
    - "transfer" inter and intra
    - "transaction history"
- "transaction" class for logging transactions
